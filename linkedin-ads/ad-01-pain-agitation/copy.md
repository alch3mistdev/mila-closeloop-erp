## Hook Strategy
Names the specific operational frustration of incomparable cross-plant data. Grounded in a former plant finance leader's description of North American manufacturing facility vs. Mexico plant costing discrepancies. The "12 plants on 6 ERPs" opener is concrete and instantly recognizable to anyone living this reality.

**Psychological triggers used:**
- **Pattern recognition** — Target audience immediately sees their own situation
- **Loss aversion** — "Don't actually consolidate" implies hidden risk they haven't addressed
- **Specificity** — Numbers (12, 6) signal expertise, not generic marketing
