## Hook Strategy
Leverages the SAP ECC 2027 standard support deadline (extended to 2030 for qualifying customers) as an external forcing function validated in research. This creates legitimate urgency tied to an industry-wide timeline that the target audience is already aware of — it's not manufactured scarcity. The "Ready?" kicker transforms awareness of the deadline into self-assessment of readiness.

**Psychological triggers used:**
- **Legitimate urgency** — Tied to a real, externally imposed deadline (not manufactured)
- **FOMO** — Implied risk of being unprepared while peers are already acting
- **Temporal anchoring** — "2027" creates a concrete mental deadline
- **Contrarian reframing** — "Stopgap, not a strategy" challenges the extended-support safety net
- **Loss aversion** — "Years of workarounds" frames the cost of inaction
